Thinktum Mural (on hold)

Duration: Jan 2020 - Present

Size: 24' x 8'

Software Used: Figma, Procreate

Overview

This mural was a design that a close friend and I developed over the past few months for Thinktum Inc., an innovative company based in Waterloo. At the beginning of January 2020, we were approached by Thinktum Inc. to design a mural for their office. We visited their office multiple times to discuss ideas and expectations with the co-founders about what they envisioned their mural to represent. We were faced with the challenge of creating a mural that exemplifies Thinktumâ€™s values that they stood for in a physical space. The Co-Founders wanted a mural designed to represent the company, what they stood for, and a design that showcased the atmosphere of the company.

The Design Process

Over the course of a few months, we visited Thinktum's office several times to discuss ideas with the employees and the Co-Founders. While we were at the office, we were able to see the wall where the mural will be painted, and we were able to take notes about the interior of the office. After having discussions with the Co-Founders about the company's values and vision for this mural, we started brainstorming for ideas.

We came up with two designs that we felt represented all their values and personality well. The Co-Founders and employees loved both designs but couldn't decide which design they wanted to move forward with. A solution that we suggested was that we could combine both designs into one cohesive mural, with both aspects of the two designs included. We wanted to incorporate the colours that were on the floors at the office onto our design, and ultimately, that is how our current design came to be. We felt that having these colours could unify the mural and the interior of the office.

Next Steps
