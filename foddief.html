Foodie Venture

Date: Fall 2019

Software Used: Adobe XD

Overview

Foodie Venture was a project that I developed for my Fine 229 class: Hybrid Digital Media. As a class, we created a persona for a Waterloo student, and based on the persona we made; we had to develop an app for that student.

Background

As a student myself, I thought about the types of problems that I faced while being a Waterloo student. One day I was walking to the library after class, and I got hungry. I couldn't decide what to eat. The problem was that I had no idea what some of the stores on campus had on their menusâ€”not knowing what type of food was being sold made it more difficult for me to decide what I wanted to eat and where. With this problem in mind, i chose to create an app that I felt could help other students who may experience the same problem.

User Research

To find the best solution, and provide the best experience for the end-users for this product, I created individual user personas for different levels of students, first-year to fourth-year students.

My Goals

After having done my research, I created some goals that I hoped to establish with my design:

  • Easy navigation
  • Allow Users not to think
  • Easy to get the Information

The Design Process

Before starting the digital wireframes, I began my design using paper and pen. Starting with pen and paper was an important step because I needed to design the overall flow and design of the app before moving onto the digital wireframes. I decided to go with a design where the user can scroll and select the choice of food, and it would say where the user can purchase it on campus.

App Branding

I chose the colour blue as the primary base because the colour blue represents reliability, and my product is a reliable source when it comes to finding a place to buy food on campus. I used yellow as a secondary colour because it represents happiness and to me, food brings me happiness, that's why I chose yellow.
