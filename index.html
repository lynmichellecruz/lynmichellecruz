About

My name is Lyn Michelle Cruz, and I'm a multimedia designer based in Waterloo! I'm studying Fine Arts and Business and specializing in Digital Art with a Computing Option at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I'm working as a UX Design Developer at lleTTonna and as a Social Media Coordinator at the Entrepreneurship Society.

As a young artist and designer who has a passion for art, design, and technology, I'm always looking for opportunities to integrate my passions to build beautiful and useful products. I'm hoping to find opportunities to learn more about publication layout design, UX/UI design and product design.

When I'm not working or studying, you can find me drawing, painting, reading, eating, and spending time with my friends and family.
