MomsOverMatter

Role:UX Design Developer

Duration: April 2020 - Present

Software Used: Figma

Overview

MomsOverMatter is a medical information app that allows mothers to access personalized treatment options for PPD and related disorders based on user-generated responses. MomsOverMatter will become available for IOS, Android, and Web. MomsOverMatter is a project that I am working on at lleTTonna. I am a User Experience Designer for the MomsOverMatter Web Applications team consisting of 3 UX/UI designers and 5 developers.

This project is currently in the early stages of development. We are currently finishing up our low-fidelity Android and web application wireframe and are beginning the high-fidelity wireframes for IOS. My team and I often interact with developers and the project manager to ensure the success of deliverables.

Next Steps

  • Complete low-fidelity wireframes.
  • Create high-fidelity wireframes for Android and web applications.

Willow

Software: Figma

Meet Willow, the official mascot for lleTTonna's MomsOverMatter and MomsInMind application.

At lleTTonna, several apps are in the works of development, and I got the opportunity to design the mascot for those apps. I had created five versions of the mascot using lleTTonna's brand colours. As a result, this is the design that we feel will be the best fit for connecting with our target user, mothers.
