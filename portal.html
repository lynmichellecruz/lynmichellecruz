SYDE Comics

Date: Winter 2020

Size: 8" x 11"

Software Used: Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Procreate

Earlier this term, I was filming a video series of frequently asked questions for the Department of Systems Design Engineering; however, due to Covid-19, I was unable to continue creating those videos. A solution that my supervisor and I came up with was to make comics answering those frequently asked questions that we weren't able to film.

The comic was created for the Systems Design Engineering open house for future terms. The comic essentially answers the question, "Why did you decide to study Systems Design Engineering? What is your favourite thing about it?"

The content and visuals were inspired by the various topics taught in Systems Design Engineering and the iconic gooses seen at the University of Waterloo campus. With this comic, I was aiming for something fun, and that would catch the eyes of prospective students when receiving this comic during Open House.

The process of creating this comic involved creating several rough drafts made with Procreate for iPad. The creation of the comic assets was made with Adobe Illustrator and assembled with Adobe InDesign.
